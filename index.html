The Staff

For every scene, your AI staff generates 3 variants — one plays it safe, one takes risks, one goes wildcard. You don't write the first draft. You direct the room.

The Story Bible

Your Codex tracks every character, relationship, world rule, and plot thread. When the staff pitches a scene, they check the bible first. Dead characters stay dead.

The Governor

The Narrative Governor catches problems before you see them: continuity breaks, voice drift, missing arc beats. If a draft violates your canon, it gets flagged and regenerated.
