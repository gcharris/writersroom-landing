Common Questions

Do I need NotebookLM?

No. You can start with plain text or just your ideas. NotebookLM is optional for power users who want to connect their existing research library.

Is Telegram really free?

Yes. The Telegram studio is our free tier with 30 scenes per month. Upgrade to Creator for the full web Workbench and more scenes.

What does "use your own API keys" mean?

Showrunner users can connect their own OpenAI, Claude, or Gemini API keys to control costs and use their preferred AI models. This is optional — our included AI works great for most users.
